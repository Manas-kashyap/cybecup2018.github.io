About Cyber Cup

♦ ♦ ♦




 CYBER CUP, the annual technical festival of Amity University, Noida curated on behalf of the Department of Computer Science of Amity School of Engineering and Technology aims to appeal to the Tech enthusiasts in the form of a series of events enveloping various technological terrains with events such as BlendIt for strengthening the designing skills, HandsOn to make the participants aware of the latest widgets & gizmos and CodeIt, a platform to present their ideas and work along with many others.
Cyber Cup concurrently helps tech fanatics in exploring their capabilities and indulging in competitive contests accompanied by numerous fun elements perpetuating the upbeat spirit of a festival.

About Amity

♦ ♦ ♦

A place where dreams are germinated, nourished and fulfilled, with it’s rich endowment and storied history, AMITY proffers wings coordinated to fly and reach beyond the dreams of the future.
Amity is recognised by the Government of India and the degrees awarded here are recognised by UGC. Amity has been given the status of an ‘A’ grade university by NAAC. It’s sprawling campus of 60 acres has a footfall of more than 1.5 lakh budding students from all over India. It routinely takes up top spots in the list of prestigious universities. There are many factors that distinguishes Amity from all other universities. One of them being; high concentration of talented teachers, students and researchers. Another factor is it’s cluster of brilliance, autonomy and leadership. It is one of those world class universities that aims to render it’s students not only with all kinds of facilities and infrastructure but acts as a helping hand to fully utilize these available resources to it’s optimum level, which transforms aspirations of striving students to reach the pinnacle of success.

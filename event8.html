Highlights of the event:

Phase 1: Presentation

All the participants have to deliver their ideas to the audience and investors within a time constraint of 7 minutes.

After every Presentation the investors will ask a few questions of interest related to their startups. The investors can also fund if they like the idea of the establishment of the participating company.
